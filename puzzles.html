I watch anime, read light novels, play video games, the normal things any high-schooler does. If there's one thing about me that's different, I guess it's that I like puzzlehunts more than the average person.

What's a puzzlehunt, you ask? Well, a puzzlehunt is --[2 pages of omitted exposition showing I like puzzlehunts way more than the average person]--

Ah, I guess I won't be able to participate in the next Mystery Hunt... That was the last thought that passed through my mind before everything went black.

Overworld

Chapter 1

I wake up, and realize I'm not on the street from before. I'm not in a hospital bed. I'm not even resting with my head on the lap of a beautiful cryptic crossword. No, I'm in the middle of a grassy field, with very oddly-shaped mountains and trees.

What's this? Is this that thing that happens to protagonists in light novels when they save the kid from a truck? Have I been... sent to another world?

Hoping against hope, I say out loud "Status", and sure enough, a display pops up in front of me, showing things like my name, my level, and my skills.

Skills!

Well, I said skills, but there's only one. What's this?

-------------------------------------------
Blessing of Orpheus

Every time you solve a puzzle, gain a level
-------------------------------------------

What?! Isn't this skill perfect for me? As I look over this skill, I realize I can feel some puzzles, in a village I can see off in the distance.

I immediately understand my purpose here. I must have been reincarnated in order to save this world from its Demon Lord, or something like that. Well in that case, I'll need to strengthen myself. I start off towards the village I spotted, both to level up and to find out about this new world.
