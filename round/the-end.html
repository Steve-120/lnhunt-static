The End

Chapter 3

I work my way through a stronghold and put the eyes of ender into an end portal, then I hop in and get ready for my final battle. But as soon as I'm on the other side, a wave of pressure hits me. Is that... The Ender Dragon? They're nowhere close, but I can feel their puzzling intent spread across The End. This must be one of the kai'i, the Solving Anomalies I heard the villagers whisper about.

Nope, no way, there's not a chance in Nether I could beat that thing as I am now. If I want to have even the slimmest of possibilities, I'll need to level myself up with the puzzles in this area first.
