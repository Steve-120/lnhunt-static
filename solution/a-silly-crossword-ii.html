Some of the clues have "(+XX)" at the end where XX is a number. It turns out, the answer to each of these clues contains a hidden word.

Formal shoe shine? (+41)PUMP LUSTER
What Nico from Love Live uses to sing (+46)IDOL LARYNX
Marshite, e.g. (+19)COPPER IODIDE
Horimiya, e.g. (+39)ROMCOM MANGA
Plan A (+14)PROTOCOL ONE
Poop, e.g, at certain times (+29)DIAPER CENTER
Spice Girls, say? (+28)CUMIN USERS
Roughly half the city population (+45)URBAN GIRLS

These hidden words are symbols you'd usually find on a keyboard. As clued by the title, we should take the ASCII values of these symbols. Then, we apply the shifts from the "(+XX)" to obtain a new ASCII character.

The answer is TRASHBIN.
