Solution: Lost in Translation
Answer: HAMBURGER

Written by Rio Futaba

This puzzle is about wasei eigo, which are Japanese expressions that are based on English words but do not have the same meaning as in English. The clues at the top correspond to English words and the clues at the bottom correspond to Japanese words. The Japanese words can be matched with the English words such that they have (roughly) the same reading but different meanings (e.g. CONSENT = コンセント (konsento)). The clues on top are also sorted in alphabetical order in the answers.

Clue Answer

President of the United States TRUMP

To extract, we use the first number as index into the Japanese words and shift the katakana by the second number following the katakana table (e.g. カ + 2 = ク).

Reading the extracted katakana, we get コタエハハンバーガー, which means “The answer is HAMBURGER”.
