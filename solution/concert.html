Solution: Concert
Answer: CINDERELLA

Written by lightbulbMEOW

The first step is to identify the images. It turns out, each of these images is cluing the title of a vocaloid song. Furthermore, each of these songs features exactly two vocaloids, as clued by the flavortext.

To extract, notice that the different color shapes at the top represent each vocaloid's color. Since each song features two vocaloids, we extract the letter using semaphore.

Song name Vocaloid Semaphore letter
Drop Pop Candy Kagamine Rin and Megurine Luka R
Like, Dislike Kagamine Rin and Kagamine Len O
Magnet Hatsune Miku and Megurine Luka M
Aniimo GUMI and Kamui Gakupo E
Electric Angel Kagamine Rin and Kagamine Len O
Matryoshka GUMI and Hatsune Miku A
Supermarket Fever Otomachi Una and Hatsune Miku N
Indulging: Idol Syndrome MAYU and GUMI D
Go Google It Magurine Luka and Kamui Gakupo W
Seraphim On The Ring Hatsune Miku and Kagamine Rin H
Maid Factor Kagamine Rin and Kagamine Len O

We extract the phrase "ROMEO AND WHO". At first glance the answer might be JULIET, but there's an enumeration (10) at the bottom for the answer. The cluephrase is actually another reference to a vocaloid song, Romeo and Cinderella. The answer is CINDERELLA.
