Solution: Workout Routine
Answer: CARTWHEEL HAT

Written by kt3, lightbulbMEOW

The starting positions decode to WATCH ZUIIKIN using the dancing men cipher. This is referring to to Zuiikin’ English, a Japanese TV show that combines English lessons with gymnastic exercises.

Step 1 of each exercise routine is cluing an English phrase from the show. In order of the puzzle, these phrases are:

Step 1 English phrase
Prepare to ask someone to seek urgent medical help. Call an ambulance please
Prepare to impersonate Arnold Schwarzenegger. Hasta la vista baby
Prepare to respond to someone who insulted you personally. How dare you say such a thing to me
Prepare to state your medicinal restrictions. I am allergic to penicillin
Prepare to express your bowel problems. I have a bad case of diarrhea
Prepare to confess your feelings and try to advance your relationship with someone. I want us to be more than just friends.
Prepare to explain how you lost your wallet. I was robbed by two men.
Prepare to look for someone who communicates in a certain language. Is there anyone who speaks Japanese?
Prepare to acknowledge the opportune time to play a sport. Lovely golf weather today
Prepare to offer your belongings. Take anything you want, spare me my life
Prepare to express which part you liked about the movie you just watched. The climax scene really got to me
Prepare to compliment someone's attire. You look sensational in that dress.

These phrases are in alphabetical order, which suggests we need to reorder them in some way.

All the other steps represent a step in the Zuiikin’ English videos, but one of them is just called “Rest”. The final step is to realize the “Advanced Routine” at the bottom contains these missing “Rest” steps from the routines, which provides the final ordering.

To extract, look at how many steps the “Recommendation” takes, then index that into the English phrase. The answer is CARTWHEEL HAT.

Step “Advanced Routine” step English phrase Index Letter
1 Extend your right foot forwards and slightly bend your left knee; at the same time, raise both arms as if surrendering. How dare you say such a thing to me 16 C
2 Stretch your body by raising both arms and touching your hands together. Call an ambulance please 19 A
3 Put your right hand on your hip and form a V with your left arm, then face slightly to the left. The climax scene really got to me 15 R
4 Make an X with your arms. I was robbed by two men. 13 T
5 Point both upper arms forward with your hands closed. Take anything you want 16 W
6 Extend your arms diagonally, with left arm pointing upwards and right arm pointing downwards. Is there anyone who speaks Japanese? 15 H
7 Bring your forearms sideways, keeping elbows level to your shoulders. I want us to be more than just friends. 27 E
8 Flex your two arms sideways, pointing downward, with both arms shoulder-level. You look sensational in that dress. 27 E
9 Bend your knees and reach for them with both arms. I am allergic to penicillin 21 L
10 Slouch your back and bring both arms parallel to the ground and at stomach level. I have a bad case of diarrhea 21 H
11 Throw a right straight. Hasta la vista baby 12 A
12 Pull your hips and arms together. Lovely golf weather today 14 T
