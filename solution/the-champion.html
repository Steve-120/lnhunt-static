Solution: The Champion
Answer: DREAM LUCK

Written by AndrescuIII, kt3, lightbulbmeow, spacewalker

We are presented with flavortext in the puzzle page. There are also icons in the round page that are clued to be relevant, and for every solve in this round, we are also rewarded with a named badge.

We are prompted to “add something” and to make a “full circle”.

The main aha of this metapuzzle is that adding a word after the types allows us to make semantic links with our feeders. Some particular break-ins would be browsing through UMA MUSUMEs and seeing Grass Wonder or narrowing down UTILITY to Electric Company and Water Works. Since the type associated with each puzzle is different from the type it can be semantically linked with, we can make a cycle. As for where to start reading, we thankfully have a puzzle that tells us (although this insight is not important to get to the answer).

Extracting the eigenletters between the added word and the badge, we get DREAM LUCK, the “skill” we need to have enough blaze rods and ender pearls.

Author’s Notes

This metapuzzle has unfortunate rabbitholes that walled many teams, such as the type chart, the 9 remaining types, and some other ways to “add something to something”. In hindsight, the flavortext could have still been improved.
