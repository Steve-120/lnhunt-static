Solution: when the puzzle explains the joke in the video
Answer: SCYTHE

Written by kt3

By looking up some of the more conspicuous parts, such as “900 pinecones” or “Now With Wheat!”, we can identify that these are all referencing popular skits by YouTuber ProZD.
