Consulting the flavortext, we want a “key order on what was” and to “recalibrate [our] orientation” after shining “a new light on our past accomplishments”.

Looking at the distribution of the what-was letters allows us to realize a key insight: the Overworld feeders have XMVZNBC, the bottom row of the keyboard rearranged! The Nether and The End feeders likewise are the middle (home! but unfortunately not relevant) and top rows of the keyboard rearranged. This gives us a good way to sort.

The next illuminating idea is that each typo shines a light on our past accomplishments by highlighting a letter that uniquely appears in each answer. Finally, using our Home hovertexts as positional offsets, we can recalibrate the indexing to get our final phrase, PUSH TRUCK-KUN OUT VIA STRENGTH, revealing a Mew who offers to help us return home.
