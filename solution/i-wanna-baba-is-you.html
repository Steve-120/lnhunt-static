Answer: BATTLESHIP

Written by lightbulbMEOW, sWIfT

All the levels can be completed as seen in this video:

Once at least 10 levels are solved, reading the capital letters gives ANSWER BATTLESHIP.

Author’s Notes

I made this game back in 2020. It was also the first ever game I made in JavaScript, I hope it wasn't too buggy. This game is inspired by Baba Is You and I Wanna Be The Guy fangames.

In case you want to make your own I Wanna Baba Is You levels, here is the level editor I used: https://lightbulbmeow.github.io/games/baba/editor.html

Originally there's going to be even more cursed stuff like, being able to form WINDOW IS PUSH which made it possible for Baba to literally push the window screen (like LEVEL IS PUSH in Baba Is You). There's also new properties like BOUNCE which just made objects bounce, ANTIGRAV which just reverses the gravity of an object, and ZA WARUDO which allowed some objects to move even while the game is paused. You can still access these words in the level editor.
