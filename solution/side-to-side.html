Solution: Side to Side
Answer: MONITOR

Written by kt3, InternetPerson10, lightbulbMEOW

We are greeted with a series of audio files. The first step is to identify the songs played in each audio file.

To solve this puzzle, we might notice that the flavortext is a reference to Minecraft. More specifically, the puzzle title "Side to Side" and "aw, man" in the flavortext is a direct reference to Revenge by CaptainSparklez, a Minecraft parody song.

The song Revenge is a parody of Usher's DJ Got Us Fallin' In Love, which is one of the audio files of this puzzle. It turns out, all the songs in the audio files appear in some Minecraft parody song.

To make them easier to identify, the parody songs chosen here are among the most viewed ones on Youtube. For further confirmation, the names of these Minecraft parody songs fit the blanks at the bottom of the page and appear alphabetically.

Finally, to extract, notice that each audio file has a portion of it cut out. If we play the original song and the Minecraft parody song side to side (pun intended), the cut out portion matches with exactly one word from the lyrics of the Minecraft parody song.
